FAQ

How do I create a note?

Use the quick note input at the top of the Inbox screen. Type your note and press Enter to save.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

How do I backup my notes?

Go to Settings and use the Backup feature to export your notes as a file.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your Pro subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store account. Go to your device Settings > Subscriptions to manage or cancel.

How do I enable biometric lock?

Go to Settings > Security and enable the biometric lock toggle. You will need Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint set up on your device.
