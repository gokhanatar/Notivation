Privacy Policy

Notivation ("we", "our", "the app") is a note-taking and decision-tracking application. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app. Notivation is designed as an offline-first, device-local application with no user accounts, no cloud servers, and no data collection.

1. Data Collection

Notivation does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All your notes, action items, tags, settings, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (IndexedDB).

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or account information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Crash reports sent to our servers

2. Device Permissions

Notivation may request the following device permissions, all of which are processed entirely on your device:

  • Camera & Photo Library: Used only to attach photos to your notes. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Face ID / Touch ID / Biometrics: Used to lock and protect the app. Biometric data is handled by your device's operating system and is never accessible to or stored by Notivation.
  • Notifications: Used to send you local reminders and alerts that you configure. Notifications are scheduled locally and do not involve any external push notification service.

3. Third-Party Services

Notivation does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain ads.

If you choose to enable the optional AI features, your note content may be sent to the AI provider (e.g., Google Gemini) that you configure in Settings. This is entirely opt-in, requires your own API key, and is not enabled by default.

4. In-App Purchases

Notivation offers optional Pro features through in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled securely by your device's app store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage provided by the operating system. You can protect the app with biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or device passcode).

Since your data never leaves your device, there is no risk of server-side data breaches.

6. Data Export & Deletion

  • Export: You can export all your data at any time through the app's Settings.
  • Deletion: Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all associated data. There is no cloud backup unless you manually export your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Notivation does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
