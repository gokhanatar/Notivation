Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

Notivation ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal note-taking and decision-making application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Storage

Notivation stores all your data locally on your device using IndexedDB. We do not operate any servers, cloud databases, or backend services. Your notes, decisions, action items, and all personal content never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export them.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data
  • We do not use cookies or third-party trackers
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Optional AI Features

Notivation offers optional AI-powered features (summarization, tagging, decision analysis). These features require you to provide your own API key for one of the supported services (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic Claude). When you use these features:

  • Your note content is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you selected
  • We do not process, store, or have access to this data
  • The AI provider's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data
  • AI features are entirely optional and the app works fully without them

In-App Purchases

Notivation offers Pro subscriptions through Apple App Store and Google Play Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple or Google respectively. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information.

Biometric Authentication

Notivation supports Face ID, Touch ID, and fingerprint authentication to protect your notes. Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's operating system. We never access, store, or transmit biometric data.

Camera and Photos

Notivation allows you to attach photos to your notes using your device camera or photo library. Photos are stored locally on your device as part of your note data. They are never uploaded to any server.

Local Notifications

Notivation may send local notifications for due dates, daily summaries, and follow-ups. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external server communication.

Data Export and Backup

You can export your data as JSON or Markdown files. These exports are generated locally and saved to your device. We have no access to your exported files.

Children's Privacy

Notivation is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
