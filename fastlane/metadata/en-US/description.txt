YOUR THOUGHTS DESERVE A CALM SPACE

Notivation is where clear thinking begins. Not another noisy note app — a mindful companion that helps you capture decisions, track actions, and reflect on what truly matters. Everything stays on your device, private and secure.

MORNING BRIEF — YOUR DAILY DASHBOARD
Start each day with a personalized overview of your notes, overdue tasks, and mental clarity score. The Morning Brief surfaces what needs attention, shows your momentum streak, and gently nudges you toward open loops that need closing. It adapts to your rhythm, greeting you with context-aware insights.

DECISION MAKING — THINK BEFORE YOU ACT
Decisions deserve more than a gut feeling. Create structured decision notes with weighted pros and cons, confidence tracking from 1-10, and a visual Decision Journey timeline. Watch your thinking evolve from initial spark to final outcome. The Quick Decision cards on the Decide screen let you act on pending choices with one tap.

ACTION ITEMS — FROM THOUGHT TO DONE
Every note can spawn action items with due dates. The dedicated Actions tab organizes your tasks by Today, This Week, Overdue, and Follow-up views. Check items off as you go, and watch your momentum bar grow. Notivation connects your thinking to your doing.

SMART FEATURES THAT UNDERSTAND YOU
Memory Fade gently highlights notes you have not revisited. Mind Clarity Score measures how organized your thinking is. Open Loops identifies unresolved items across all your notes. Confidence Calibration tracks how your certainty evolves over time. These are not gimmicks — they are cognitive tools.

FUTURE CAST — SCENARIO PLANNING
Explore what-if scenarios for your decisions. Create if-then branches, assign probability weights, and compare potential outcomes. Future Cast brings structured foresight to your decision-making process.

INCUBATION MODE — LET IDEAS SIMMER
Some thoughts need time. Put notes into Incubation Mode and let them rest. When the time is right, Notivation wakes them up with a gentle nudge. Great ideas often need patience, not pressure.
